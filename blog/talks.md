# Talks & Appearances

It's incredible how daunting public speaking used to be for me. After doing
it frequently over the past few years, I've become much more comfortable —
and I plan to keep going.

Most of my talks are in the space of public goods funding, regenerative
finance, and decentralised community building.

If you'd like me to speak at your event, [get in touch](/about/).

## 2026

- **[Public Goods Sustainability & ROI: Lessons from the Filecoin Impact Fund](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8r-9GjkG-7E)** @ EthCC —
  Filecoin is experimenting with new ways to sustainably fund ecosystem
  dependencies — combining capital formation, funding mechanisms, and
  impact measurement. This talk shares what's working, what's hard, and
  what the broader ecosystem can learn.
  ([recording](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8r-9GjkG-7E))
